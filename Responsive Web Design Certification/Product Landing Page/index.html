About

Gondomar RedCar Racing is a multinational team, comprising of some of the fastest sim-racers in the world. We have been at the forefront of sim-racing for over 6 months, winning major championships, advising on sim-racing and developing products with partners.

Founded over 6 months ago, we have entered and won hundreds of major titles, competing at the highest level. We are a multinational team, from Europe, the US and Asia and have a mix of real and virtual drivers. We are proudly supported by Heusinkveld, Simucube and Sim Lab.

Competition

We compete in the highest level of sim-racing esports, such as the Le Mans Virtual Series, Porsche Supercup Esports Series, IMSA Esports and Low Fuel Motorsports and are known as the most successful sim-racing team of all time across multiple titles.

Some of our achievements include getting a podium in Nurburgring in the Aston Martin GT3.
